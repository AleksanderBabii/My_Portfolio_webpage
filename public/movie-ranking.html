The Best Movies/Series According to Vita and Sasha

Vita's top 3 movies/series of all time

1. Pulp Fiction

Pulp Fiction is a 1994 American neo-noir black comedy crime film written and directed by Quentin Tarantino. It is known for its eclectic dialogue, nonlinear storyline, and satirical references to pop culture.

2. Gilmore Girls

Funny and heartwarming drama about a mother and daughter navigating life in a small town.

3. Friends

Friends is a 1994 American sitcom created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman, which aired on NBC from September 22, 1994, to May 6, 2004. The series revolves around a group of friends living in Manhattan, New York City.


Sasha's top 3 movies/series of all time

1. Naruto animated series

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

2. The Office

The Office is an American mockumentary sitcom television series that depicts the everyday work lives of office employees in the Scranton, Pennsylvania branch of the Dunder Mifflin Paper Company.

3. Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is an American neo-Western crime drama television series created and produced by Vince Gilligan. The series aired on AMC from January 20, 2008, to September 29, 2013, consisting of five seasons for a total of 62 episodes.
